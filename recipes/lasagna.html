The Perfect Lasagna!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs italian sausage
  • 3/4 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • a lot more that I don't feel like typing because this recipe is to damn long!

Steps

  1. Gather all your Ingredients
  2. Cook sausage,beef, onion and garlic in the oven over medium heat until browned
  3. Sir in crushed tomatoes, sauce and water. Season and Simmer for about 1 1/2 hours.
  4. Cook the pasta for 8 to 10 minutes.
  5. Combine Cheese with egg and salt
